The Poet

Patricia Francis is a British Army veteran, mother, and poet writing from the intersections of trauma and transcendence. Her work explores the architecture of survival—how we build homes inside our own breaking, how language becomes both weapon and wound.

Through Briar Wing Poetry, she creates spaces where the unspeakable finds form. Her verses move between the grit of lived experience and the gold of metaphor, offering readers not escape, but deeper presence.

"I write to perform, and I perform to heal—with music, with vision, with story."
