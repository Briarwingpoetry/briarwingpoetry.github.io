The Collection

Through the Thorn, Into Light

This debut collection traces the journey from shadow to sun—three movements that mirror the breaking of chains, the climbing of mountains, and the flight toward freedom. Like the rose that wraps around the bird, these poems explore how we can be both wounded and winged.

The War Nobody Saw opens in the dark—navigating neurodivergent experience, the aftermath of service, and the silence that follows trauma. The Glass Ceiling That Hung Like a Noose moves through the reckoning, the anger, the refusal to apologize for one's existence. Finally, Proof That Something Almost Stood Here arrives at the light—not as happy ending, but as hard-won dawn.

Each poem is a step from the 🥀 toward the 🕊️, from the thorn toward the sky, from the night toward the ✨.

"We are the shattered glass that still reflects light, the ruins that refuse to be called waste."

The Defiant Pen

🥀 From the Collection

I want a pen with tenacious audacity
that keeps it reluctant to fold—
ink with its own will, we spiral
through pages of a war no one else saw.

This is for the unrequited loves,
the forbidden mysteries,
the black sheep who learned
that being the threat to their hierarchy
was always the point.

Because loving herself
has become her mountain—
and she climbs with a pen
that refuses to apologize
for its sharp edges. 🕊️

Unfold

Breaking

🕊️ 2024

The chain was never as strong
as the will to be free.
I broke not in half
but in whole—
scattered like seeds
that grow in the cracks.

Watch me rise
from the architecture of almost,
from the blueprint of broken—
wings unfolding
where the thorns
used to hold me. ✨

Shattered Glass

✨ Early Work

Proof that something
almost stood here—
the architecture of almost,
the blueprint of broken.

I am the shattered glass
that still reflects light,
the ruins that refuse
to be called waste.

Build me into your mosaic,
or step carefully
around my edges.
Either way,
I remain. 🥀

We are the untended,

The Symbolism

🥀 🕊️ ✨

Briar Wing writes from the liminal spaces—the threshold where the thorn meets the wing, where the dark yields to dawn. The rose (🥀) represents the beautiful wound, the defense that becomes art, the pain that transforms rather than destroys.

The bird (🕊️) is the breaking free—not as escape, but as evolution. It carries the thorn's wisdom into the sky, understanding that flight requires both the wound and the will to rise.

The sun (✨) is not the absence of shadow, but the light that makes the shadow visible. It represents the hard-won dawn after the long night of writing, the community gathering, the hope that persists.

"From the thorn, the wing. From the wound, the word. From the night, the light."
